$ cat philosophy.md

  • ▸Declarative Determinism

    Treat your OS as a mathematical function, not an organic decaying entity. NixOS ensures environments are reproducible and time-resistant — eliminating "it works on my machine" forever.

  • ▸Plain-Text Permanence

    Local, human-readable formats over proprietary cloud silos. Markdown, plain configs, local Obsidian vaults — knowledge that's accessible decades from now with no platform dependency.

  • ▸Hardware Control & Longevity

    ThinkPads for repairability and control. LineageOS for phones. Devices that stay functional through community-driven open-source software, not vendor planned obsolescence.

  • ▸Keyboard-Driven Efficiency

    TUIs and minimalist WMs (Sway, Helix) — tools that get out of the way. Muscle memory, static reliable workflows, zero friction from heavy animations or forced UI churn.

  • ▸Anti-Fragility through Localism

    Minimize external dependencies. Declarative local configs, offline-capable tools, and self-contained environments. Infrastructure immune to internet outages and service shutdowns.
